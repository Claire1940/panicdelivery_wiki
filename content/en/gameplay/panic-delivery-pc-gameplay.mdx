<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Loop">

The core of **Panic Delivery PC gameplay** revolves around the high-stakes environment of a dystopian courier service. Players must navigate procedurally generated dungeons, complete objectives, and return to safety while managing a shared pool of 99 lives.

| Phase | Objective | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Preparation** | Shop for gear | Buy **Tracking Devices** or **Crowbars** first |
| **Infiltration** | Reach the zone | Keep the team together to avoid isolation |
| **Execution** | Secure the package | Assign one courier to carry, others to protect |
| **Extraction** | Return to base | Use **Monster Repellents** to clear exit paths |

<Callout type="tip" title="Survival Strategy">
Never let your team split up. The game punishes isolated couriers with sudden monster spawns, and since you share a pool of 99 lives, every unnecessary death drains the team's chance to complete the run.
</Callout>
