<Section icon={Swords} title="Mission Types and Objectives">

Understanding the mission structure is the first step to becoming a successful courier. Each run features randomized objectives, meaning you cannot rely on previous route knowledge alone.

| Mission Type | Primary Objective | Risk Level | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard** | Deliver packages to specific points | Moderate | Clear the route and protect the carrier |
| **Recovery** | Retrieve lost cargo across the map | High | Use tracking devices to locate targets |
| **Smuggling** | Move illicit goods with resistance | Extreme | Coordinate movement to avoid detection |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
In Recovery missions, do not split the team too far apart. If the courier holding the tracking device is isolated and eliminated, the entire team loses valuable time and lives searching for the target.
</Callout>
