<Section icon={Swords} title="Mission Types and Objectives">

Before starting any run, identify your mission type. Each category shifts the team's focus from simple navigation to specialized tasks.

| Mission Type | Primary Goal | Team Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard** | Deliver packages to the extraction point | Clear the path, protect the carrier |
| **Recovery** | Locate and secure missing cargo | Use Tracking Devices, split for search |
| **Smuggling** | Move illicit packages through zones | Use Smuggling Tools, coordinate exits |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mission Planning">
Always check the randomized objective at the start of your run. Assuming a standard delivery route when you are actually on a recovery mission is a common way to waste your team's lives.
</Callout>

</Section>
