Effective maintenance follows a specific workflow. You cannot afford to waste time in the middle of a monster-infested zone.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Damage">
    Check the terminal or tool status. If the light is blinking red, it requires immediate attention.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Area">
    Ensure no enemies are within immediate striking distance. Use a repellent if necessary.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assign Roles">
    One player performs the repair while others hold the perimeter. Never let the person repairing the equipment be the only one exposed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Verify Functionality">
    Once the repair bar is full, ensure the equipment is active before moving the package.
  </Step>
