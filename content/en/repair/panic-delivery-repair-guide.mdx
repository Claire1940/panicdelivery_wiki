<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Panic Delivery Mechanics">

In Panic Delivery, your primary goal is to deliver, recover, or smuggle packages through monster-controlled zones. Unlike traditional delivery games, your team shares a pool of 99 lives. When one courier falls, the team loses a life, and the run continues until the pool is empty or the mission is completed.

| Mechanic | Description | Impact on Run |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Shared Lives | Pool of 99 clones | Run ends when pool reaches zero |
| Procedural Maps | Random dungeon layouts | Routes change every mission |
| Mission Objectives | Standard/Recovery/Smuggling | Dictates team priority and movement |
| Shop Items | 15 available tools | Essential for surviving high-threat zones |

<Callout type="tip" title="Courier Advice">
Always check the mission type before entering the zone. A Recovery mission requires different utility tools compared to a Standard delivery run.
