<Section icon={Target} title="Mission Types and Package Logic">

Panic Delivery missions are randomized, meaning no two runs are identical. Understanding the specific requirements for each mission type is crucial for efficient completion.

| Mission Type | Primary Objective | Team Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard** | Deliver package to target | Protect the carrier at all costs |
| **Recovery** | Locate lost cargo using tracker | Split to search, regroup to transport |
| **Smuggling** | Move illicit goods safely | Avoid high-traffic monster paths |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Mission Goal">
    Identify if your mission is Standard, Recovery, or Smuggling at the start of the run.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assign Roles">
    Designate a package carrier and an escort. The carrier should not engage in direct combat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Route">
    Move through the procedurally generated dungeon, using tools to clear obstacles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Delivery">
    Secure the package at the drop-off point and prepare for the return or extraction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Cargo">
Different package sizes (Small, Medium, Large) affect your movement speed and visibility. Large packages make you a prime target for monsters, so ensure your team provides a solid defensive perimeter.
</Callout>
