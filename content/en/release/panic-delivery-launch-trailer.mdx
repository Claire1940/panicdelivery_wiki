Understanding the objective is the first step to survival. The Early Access build features three distinct mission types, each with randomized goals that prevent routes from becoming predictable.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Mission Type">
    Before starting, check if the mission is Standard, Recovery, or Smuggling. Each requires a different approach to movement and defense.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Package Strategy">
    Small, Medium, and Large packages affect your mobility. Large packages are heavy and require team support to transport safely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute and Escape">
    Complete the objective and head to the extraction point. Keep an eye on the shared life pool to ensure you don't burn through your team's resources.
  </Step>

| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Smuggling** | Protected transport of illicit goods | Extreme |
