<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Courier Essentials">

Starting your first run in Panic Delivery requires understanding the core gameplay loop. You are not just a courier; you are a survivor managing a limited pool of company-sponsored lives.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Form Your Team">
    Join solo or with up to three other players. Remember that all couriers share a single pool of 99 lives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Mission">
    Check if you are on a Standard, Recovery, or Smuggling mission. Each requires different strategies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure the Package">
    Whether it is a Small, Medium, or Large package, the carrier is the team's primary responsibility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Resources">
    Use your Speetchy to purchase tools. Prioritize survival gear before spending on cosmetic swag.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Shared Life Danger">
Running out of lives resets the run entirely. Avoid reckless exploration when the team's life pool is low, as every death significantly impacts your chances of success.
</Callout>

</Section>
