<Section icon={Map} title="Map Zones and Survival">

Panic Delivery features procedurally generated dungeons across three primary zones. Each map requires a unique approach to navigation and threat management.

| Map Zone | Theme | Primary Hazard |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scout the Layout">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assign Roles">
    Designate a carrier, a protector, and a utility handler to manage terminals or tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Regroup at Exits">

  </Step>
</Steps>
