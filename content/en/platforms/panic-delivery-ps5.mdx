<Section icon={Swords} title="Enemy Behavior and Survival">

With 13 unique enemies currently in the game, knowing when to fight and when to flee is the difference between a successful run and a total reset.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze the Threat">
    Observe the enemy's movement pattern. Aggressive types require immediate defensive measures, while others can be outrun.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Coordinate Defense">
    Use your shop items. Repellents are excellent for creating breathing room, while the Crowbar serves as a reliable emergency weapon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Regroup and Reposition">
    If the team is overwhelmed, drop the package temporarily if necessary to clear a path. Survival is the priority.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Enemy Type | Behavior Pattern | Best Counterplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Threat** | Chases player proximity | Use Repellents or outrun |
| **Lootmuncher** | Attempts to steal cargo | Stun with gadgets/weapons |
| **Environmental** | Area denial | Use traversal tools to bypass |

<Callout type="success" title="Survival Rule">
Fighting is a resource sink. Only engage enemies if they are actively blocking your path or threatening the package carrier. Otherwise, keep moving to conserve your team's shared lives.
</Callout>
